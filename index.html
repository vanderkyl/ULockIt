Storage FAQ's

Storage FAQ's

When can I move in?

If there is a storage space available that meets your specific storage needs you can move in immediately.

 

How much notice needs to be given before I move out?

We require at least 24 hour notice before you move out as long as it is prior to the rental due date.

 

What type of documentation is needed to rent?

A Government issued ID (example: driver’s license, state id, or passport)

 

Do I need to sign a long-term lease?

No, we rent on a month-to-month basis unless the customer requests otherwise.

 

Can I only access my unit when the office is open?

No. At the time of signing you will be given your own security pass code that will allow you access 7 days a week, 6:00 am to 9:00 pm.

 

How do I make a payment?

You can pay in person, mail a check or money order or make a payment through our website. You may also sign up to have payments automatically deducted from your credit card/savings/checking account monthly.

 

What forms of payments do you accept?

Checks, Money Orders, VISA, MasterCard, American Express and Discover.

Do you sell boxes and storage supplies?

Yes. We sell various moving supplies in our office.

 

How do I know how much storage space I will need?

You can either use our Storage Calculator on our website or call our manager for assistance.

 

Do I need to bring my own lock?

We have secure disc locks available for purchase at the time of the rental.

 

Are there any items that I am NOT able to store?

Liquids, Explosives, Flammable liquids, Toxic materials and Perishables. Please contact us if you have any questions regarding a specific item.

 

What is the difference between Office Hours and Gate Hours?

Office hours are hours in which a manager will be available to answer questions about rentals, payments or the facility. Gate hours are hours in which you can access your unit, whether the office is open or not.
